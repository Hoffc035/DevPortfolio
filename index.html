About Me


Hi there! My name is Chris, and I'm on an exciting journey from the kitchen to the world of web development. With years of experience as a chef, I've honed my skills in creativity, problem-solving, and attention to detail - qualities that I believe translate seamlessly into the world of coding.

My journey into web development began as a hobby, a way to explore my fascination with technology and design during my downtime. However, as I delved deeper into the world of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I found myself captivated by the endless possibilities and challenges that coding presents.

Now, at 30 years old, I've made the decision to transition from the culinary arts to a career in web development. I've dedicated countless hours to learning and refining my skills, completing online courses, building projects, and immersing myself in the vibrant developer community.

What excites me most about web development is the opportunity to create meaningful and impactful experiences for users across the globe. Whether it's crafting intuitive interfaces, optimizing performance, or solving complex technical problems, I thrive on the challenge of bringing ideas to life in the digital realm.

As I embark on this new chapter of my career, I'm eager to apply my unique blend of creativity, attention to detail, and problem-solving skills to my work as a web developer. I'm seeking opportunities to contribute to innovative projects, collaborate with talented teams, and continue growing and evolving in this dynamic field.

I'm passionate about leveraging my diverse background and experiences to make a positive impact in the world of technology, and I'm excited to see where this journey takes me next.

Let's build something amazing together!
